# User Personas

## Persona 1: The AI-Curious Freshman

### Background

- **Name**: Maya Chen
- **Age**: 18
- **Major**: Computer Science (Freshman)
- **Location**: University campus
- **Tech Savvy**: Moderate

### Goals

- Learn practical AI skills beyond classroom theory
- Build a portfolio of AI projects
- Network with peers interested in AI/tech
- Understand how AI impacts different career paths
- Get hands-on experience with AI tools

### Pain Points

- Overwhelmed by the rapid pace of AI developments
- Unsure which AI skills are most valuable for career
- Lacks practical experience with enterprise AI applications
- Difficulty finding mentorship and guidance
- Fear of falling behind peers in AI literacy

### Motivations

- Wants to be prepared for the AI-driven job market
- Excited about AI's potential but needs direction
- Seeks community of like-minded learners
- Wants to understand AI beyond the hype

### Job Club Needs

- Beginner-friendly AI learning resources
- Structured onboarding process
- Peer learning opportunities
- Project ideas suitable for beginners
- Career guidance on AI-related paths

## How Job Club Serves These Personas

### Common Needs Across All Personas

- **Community**: Safe space to learn and experiment with AI
- **Practical Skills**: Hands-on experience, not just theory
- **Career Preparation**: Understanding how AI impacts job market
- **Networking**: Connections with peers, mentors, and industry
- **Resources**: Curated learning paths and project ideas

### Differentiated Approaches

- **Freshmen**: Structured onboarding, beginner resources
- **Career Switchers**: Accelerated paths, portfolio building
- **Entrepreneurs**: Advanced discussions, showcase opportunities
